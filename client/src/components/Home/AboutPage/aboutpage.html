About Us

Welcome to Tea Shop! We are a family-owned tea shop that has been serving the community for over 20 years. Our goal is to provide our customers with the highest quality teas from around the world.

At Tea Shop, we believe that tea is more than just a beverage. It's an experience. That's why we offer a wide variety of teas to suit every taste and occasion. Whether you're looking for a classic black tea or a refreshing herbal blend, we have something for everyone.

We are committed to sustainability and source our teas from ethical and environmentally responsible suppliers. We also offer a selection of organic and fair trade teas.

Thank you for choosing Tea Shop. We look forward to serving you!
